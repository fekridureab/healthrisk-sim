Round 1 — Risk Identification

January 15, 2026 · Al-Rayyan Surgical Centre, Doha, Qatar · ASHRM ERM — Operational Domain

Al-Rayyan Surgical Centre is a 150-bed private hospital in Doha performing 4,500 surgeries per year. At 09:00 AM on January 15, 2026, the Risk Management Committee receives an urgent call. Their sole sterile supply vendor in Southeast Asia reports that their factory has been destroyed by flooding. No deliveries are possible for 6 to 8 weeks.

The supply chain manager is on annual leave. His deputy has no authority to activate emergency protocols. A Theatre Manager stock audit confirms only 4 days of sterile supplies remain. 23 elective surgeries are scheduled for next week.
