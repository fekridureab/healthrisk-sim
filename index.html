Round 1 — Risk Identification

January 15, 2026 · Al-Rayyan Surgical Centre, Doha, Qatar · ASHRM ERM — Operational Domain

Al-Rayyan Surgical Centre is a 150-bed private hospital performing 4,500 surgeries per year. At 09:00 AM, the Risk Management Committee receives an urgent call — their sole sterile supply vendor's factory in Southeast Asia was destroyed by flooding. No deliveries for 6–8 weeks. The supply chain manager is on annual leave. His deputy has NO authority to act. A stock audit reveals only 4 days of sterile supplies remain. 23 elective surgeries are scheduled next week. Mr. Al-Rashidi (age 58) awaits urgent cancer surgery on January 20.
